# Demo video — script & shot list

**Owner:** Himel
**Target length:** 3 minutes 30 seconds (max 4 min)
**Deliverable:** A single video file (`.mp4`) plus a short readme paragraph in the submission package.
**Tools:** Loom, OBS, or built-in screen recorder. Record at 1920×1080 if you can.

## Pre-record checklist
1. Warm the backend first — it sleeps on the free tier. Open https://syngenta-backend.onrender.com/health and wait for `{"status":"ok"}` before you start recording.
2. Open https://frontend-pi-rose-30.vercel.app/ in incognito so localStorage is fresh.
3. Confirm `EN` is selected in the language picker.
4. Have these URLs ready in separate tabs to switch quickly:
   - `/` (landing)
   - `/dashboard`
   - `/retailer/RTL_00001?repId=REP_0001`
   - `/anomalies`
   - `/reps`
5. Close any noisy desktop apps. Mute Slack / Discord notifications.

## Scene 1 — Hook (0:00 – 0:25)
**Show:** Landing page hero. Slowly scroll through it once.
**Voice:**
> "Syngenta has thousands of field representatives across India, visiting retailers in over 600 districts. Today, the daily plan is built on routine — a fixed retailer rotation. But Indian agriculture isn't routine. Pest outbreaks happen in weeks. Weather shifts in days. So Syngenta wants something better. We built Disha."

## Scene 2 — The promise (0:25 – 0:55)
**Show:** Scroll through "Why this matters" stats, then pause briefly on the capability cards.
**Voice:**
> "Disha — Hindi for direction — turns the rep's day into a ranked, explainable plan that adapts to inventory, weather, pest pressure, and farmer demand. Four AI layers, working together. Let me show you what the rep sees."

## Scene 3 — The daily plan (0:55 – 1:35)
**Show:** Click "View today's plan". Land on `/dashboard`. Pause on the map view briefly. Then hover over the polyline.
**Voice:**
> "The rep opens the app and sees today's stops on a map, sequenced by priority and by route. Urgent retailers are red, high-priority amber, normal grey. The system has already detected 19 active alerts in the territory and used them to bias the route — stops with open stock-outs are visited earlier. The total drive is 11.97 kilometres, 17 percent shorter than a naïve route."

## Scene 4 — Point of visit (1:35 – 2:30)
**Show:** Click a pin → "Open retailer" → land on retailer detail. Scroll to "AI Next Best Action", click the button, wait for the response to stream in.
**Voice:**
> "At each stop, the rep gets a point-of-visit briefing — generated by Gemini, grounded in this retailer's actual context: stock state, recent sales, open alerts, what nearby growers have been clicking on WhatsApp. The Random Forest below gives a separate ML pick with a confidence score, and the LSTM velocity chart projects when the next stock-out will hit. The rep walks in informed."

## Scene 5 — Outcome learning (2:30 – 2:55)
**Show:** Scroll to outcome logger. Click "Sale made", type a product, click Submit. Wait for the green confirmation. Click back to dashboard. The retailer should be missing from the list.
**Voice:**
> "After the visit, the rep logs the outcome. As soon as it's saved, the stop drops off today's plan. Tomorrow, that outcome flows back into the model so the next day's recommendations are tuned by what actually converted."

## Scene 6 — Language switch (2:55 – 3:10)
**Show:** Click the language picker (globe icon, top right). Pick हिंदी. Pan slowly across the dashboard as the Hindi loads.
**Voice:**
> "Every screen ships in English and Hindi, auto-detected from the rep's state. Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, and Marathi are a one-file addition each."

## Scene 7 — Close (3:10 – 3:30)
**Show:** Final landing CTA section ("See the day reorganised in real time"). Hold on the green CTA strip.
**Voice:**
> "Disha is a Progressive Web App, installable on any phone, works on patchy connectivity. We've shipped the full prototype on Vercel and Render — and we'd love to take it into a ninety-day pilot. Team RUSH, IIT Madras × Syngenta Hackathon 2026."
